# Dwarf clicker

---
## Initial state of play

### Player
- Player has `1` dwarf.

### World
- Each day, player has a `5%` chance of gaining a random new dwarf.

---
## Gameplay

### Player actions
- The player is able to assign dwarves to tasks:
